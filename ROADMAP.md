# Hambry 90-Day Growth Roadmap

## The Core Challenge

The sitemap was submitted to Google on February 27, 2026. Google's indexing timeline is 2–4 weeks, and building domain authority from zero takes months. Waiting for organic search alone will miss 30-day targets. The strategy: drive traffic through immediate channels while SEO compounds in the background.

---

## Days 1–30: Acceleration Plays

### Play 1 — Reddit (Free, Immediate, High Ceiling)
Reddit is the fastest way to put Hambry content in front of tens of thousands of people immediately. Target subreddits: `r/nottheonion`, `r/politics`, `r/technology`, `r/humor`. The key is not spamming links — find the 5–10 subreddits where Hambry articles genuinely fit and participate as a real account. One viral Reddit post can drive 10,000–50,000 visitors in a single day.

**Cost:** $0
**Potential:** Day 30 traffic target in a single post

### Play 2 — Threads and Bluesky
These platforms have growing audiences that skew toward satire consumers — educated, media-savvy, looking for sharp commentary. Bluesky in particular has a strong ex-Twitter crowd. Mirror the X posting engine to both platforms. Same content, three distribution channels, near-zero marginal cost.

**Cost:** $0
**Action:** Set up accounts and wire into the X posting engine this week

### Play 3 — Backlink Outreach (SEO Accelerant)
Submit Hambry to every "satirical news sites" list, media directory, and web directory that accepts submissions. Targets include:
- FeedSpot's Top 30 Satirical News Sites
- Wikipedia's list of satirical news websites
- University LibGuides

Getting listed on even a few of these dramatically accelerates domain authority. Grunt work that compounds forever.

**Cost:** $0

### Play 4 — Headline-Only X Strategy
The highest-performing satire accounts on X post standalone jokes and observations that go viral on their own — not just article links. Hambry should post 5–10 standalone satirical takes per day with no links. Build the brand voice, attract followers, create engagement. When followers see an article link mixed in later, they click because they already trust the voice. Reference: Babylon Bee has 4.8M followers largely because their tweets work as standalone comedy.

**Cost:** $0
**Action:** Add standalone tweet mode to X automation

### Play 5 — "Best of Hambry" Weekly Digest
Curate the top 10 headlines and articles from the week into a single shareable piece — a blog post, a thread, or a newsletter issue. Creates a second content format from existing material at zero production cost. Foundation for the newsletter.

**Cost:** $0

---

## Days 30–60: Compounding Phase

By Day 30, analytics data reveals what's actually resonating — which categories, headline styles, and topics drive clicks. Stop guessing, start optimizing.

### Play 6 — Double Down on Winners
Whatever category or topic is driving the most traffic, increase production in that area. If politics articles get 10x the traffic of sports articles, shift the content mix. The daily article count stays constant but allocation changes based on real data.

### Play 7 — Google News Publisher Application
Once a few weeks of consistent publishing and indexed content exist, apply to Google News as a publisher. Acceptance surfaces articles directly in the News tab and Google Discover feeds on mobile. Satire sites are eligible if clearly labeled as satire. High-impact, zero-cost move.

**Cost:** $0
**Prerequisite:** Several weeks of consistent publishing + indexed content

### Play 8 — Cross-Promotion with Other Satire Accounts
Engage with smaller satire accounts on X, Threads, and Bluesky. Repost their content, comment on their takes, build genuine relationships. The satire community is small and collaborative. A mutual shoutout from an account with 10,000 followers outperforms 100 posts into the void.

---

## Days 60–90: Monetization Preparation

### Play 9 — Ad Network Applications
- **Google AdSense:** Apply as soon as consistent traffic exists, even if modest
- **Mediavine:** Apply if hitting 50,000+ monthly sessions (significantly higher RPMs)

Application process takes 2–4 weeks — starting at Day 60 means revenue could be flowing by Day 90.

### Play 10 — Merch Store Setup (Days 60–75)
Set up a print-on-demand store integrated into the site (Shopify or equivalent, zero inventory cost). Curate the 10 best headlines onto t-shirts, mugs, and stickers. Not about volume initially — about having a revenue stream live and proving the model works.

### Play 11 — Sponsor Outreach
Once any meaningful audience numbers exist, begin reaching out to potential newsletter and site sponsors. Target: small brands, indie products, and tools that align with a smart, engaged readership.
